Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Privacy Highlights

  • Your financial data stays on your device
  • We never sell your personal information
  • Minimal data collection for app functionality
  • Delete your data anytime from settings

1. Information We Collect

Data You Provide: Portfolio data (assets, quantities, prices) that you manually enter into the App. This data is stored locally on your device.

Automatically Collected: Device information, app usage analytics, and crash reports to improve app performance and stability.

Third-Party Services: When fetching market data, your device may connect to Stooq and Alpha Vantage APIs. These requests include ticker symbols only. If you choose to use AI insights, the app may also connect to OpenAI and send an aggregated portfolio summary (no personal identifiers).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Fetch market prices for your tracked assets
  • Send notifications you've opted into
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Process subscription payments (via App Store/Play Store)

3. Data Storage & Security

Local Storage: Your portfolio data is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage. We do not have access to your financial data.

Cloud Sync: If you enable cloud backup, data is encrypted end-to-end before being stored in secure cloud infrastructure.

Security Measures: We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption, secure API connections, and regular security audits.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Apple App Store / Google Play - Payment processing
  • RevenueCat - Subscription management
  • Stooq - Stock market data
  • Alpha Vantage - FX and crypto data
  • OpenAI - Optional AI-generated portfolio insights

Each service has its own privacy policy that governs their use of your data.

5. Your Rights

  • Access - Request a copy of your data
  • Correction - Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion - Delete your data from the App
  • Portability - Export your data in common formats
  • Opt-out - Disable analytics and notifications

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Data Retention

Local data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App. Analytics data is retained for up to 2 years for performance analysis.

8. International Users

If you access the App from outside the United States, your data may be transferred to and processed in countries with different data protection laws. By using the App, you consent to such transfers.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or via email.
