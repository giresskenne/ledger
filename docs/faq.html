General

What is Ledger?

Ledger is a private portfolio tracker for stocks, crypto, real estate, and other assets. It helps you monitor your net worth with real-time market data, all stored securely on your device.

Is Ledger free?

Yes! Ledger offers a generous free tier with core features. Premium unlocks advanced analytics, AI insights, unlimited assets, and cloud backup for $4.99/month or $39.99/year.

Do I need to create an account?

Nope! Ledger works completely offline without any account, email, or personal information. Just download and start tracking.

What devices does Ledger support?

Ledger is available for iPhone and iPad running iOS 14 or later. Android and web versions are in development.

Privacy & Security

Where is my data stored?

All your portfolio data is stored locally on your device. We never upload your holdings, quantities, or purchase prices to our servers. Premium users can optionally enable encrypted cloud backup.

Can you see my portfolio?

Absolutely not. Your financial data stays on your device. We have no access to what you own, how much you paid, or your net worth.

Features

What asset types can I track?

Stocks, ETFs, mutual funds, cryptocurrency, real estate, vehicles, savings accounts, collectibles, and custom assets. If it has value, you can track it in Ledger.

Do prices update automatically?

Yes! Stocks and crypto with ticker symbols get automatic price updates during market hours. Manual assets (real estate, cars) require manual value updates.

Can I track multiple portfolios?

Currently, Ledger supports one consolidated portfolio per device. We're considering multi-portfolio support for a future update.

How accurate are the prices?

We use industry-standard APIs (Stooq, Alpha Vantage) with 5-15 minute delays for stocks and real-time data for crypto. Accuracy is high, but verify with your broker for critical decisions.

Troubleshooting

Prices aren't updating. What do I do?

Check your internet connection, pull down to refresh, and verify ticker symbols are correct. If issues persist, email contact.corpltd@gmail.com with the asset details.

I can't find my stock ticker

Try searching with the exchange suffix (e.g., "BP.L" for London Stock Exchange). If it still doesn't appear, you can skip ticker search and manually enter values.

The app crashed. Did I lose my data?

Your data is saved automatically after each change. Crashes don't cause data loss. If you experience frequent crashes, please report to contact.corpltd@gmail.com.

How do I backup my data?

Free users: Settings > Export Data to create CSV/JSON backups. Premium users: Enable cloud sync in Settings for automatic encrypted backups.

Account & Subscription

How do I restore purchases on a new device?

Open Ledger, go to Settings > Subscription, and tap "Restore Purchases". Your Premium access will sync via App Store.

Can I use Premium on multiple devices?

Yes! Premium is tied to your Apple ID. Use it on all your iOS devices (iPhone, iPad) signed into the same account.

I'm being charged but don't see Premium features

Try Settings > Subscription > Restore Purchases. If issues persist, email contact.corpltd@gmail.com with your purchase receipt.

Data & Export

Can I import data from other apps?

Premium users can import CSV files. Format: Name, Ticker, Quantity, Purchase Price, Current Value. See documentation for template.

What format is the export?

CSV (spreadsheet-compatible) and JSON (developer-friendly). Both formats include all your holdings with full details.

Can I sync between iPhone and iPad?

Yes, with Premium cloud backup enabled. Changes sync automatically across devices on the same Apple ID.
