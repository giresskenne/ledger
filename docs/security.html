What Data We Collect

What we DO collect:

  • Minimal crash reports (device type, OS version, error logs)
  • Anonymous app usage statistics (feature adoption, not your holdings)
  • Ticker symbols when fetching market data (never linked to you)

What we DON'T collect:

  • Your asset holdings, quantities, or purchase prices
  • Your net worth or portfolio value
  • Your name, email, phone number (unless you contact support)
  • Your location, contacts, or photos
  • Your browsing history or app activity outside Ledger

Market Data Privacy

When you add stocks or crypto with ticker symbols, Ledger fetches prices from public APIs (Stooq, Alpha Vantage). These requests include:

  • Ticker symbol only (e.g., "AAPL", "BTC")
  • No user identifiers
  • No quantity or value data
  • No personally identifiable information

The API providers cannot tie these requests back to you or your portfolio.

Cloud Backup Security

Premium Feature: Optional encrypted cloud sync

How it works:

  1. Your portfolio data is encrypted on your device using AES-256
  2. Only the encrypted blob is uploaded to secure cloud storage
  3. The decryption key never leaves your device
  4. We cannot read your data‚Äîonly you can decrypt it

Cloud backup requires iOS Keychain (backed by Apple's security) to sync encryption keys across your devices.

Best Security Practices

  • Enable Face ID/Touch ID in Settings for app lock
  • Export backups regularly to safeguard against device loss
  • Use a strong device passcode (6+ digits, not easily guessable)
  • Keep iOS updated for latest security patches
  • Don't jailbreak your device‚Äîit weakens security protections

Data Deletion

You own your data. Delete it anytime:

  • Individual assets: Swipe left on any holding and tap Delete
  • All data: Settings > Clear All Assets (Testing) - permanent deletion
  • App removal: Uninstalling Ledger erases all local data
  • Cloud backup: Deleted automatically when subscription ends

Third-Party Services

Ledger integrates with minimal third parties, each with their own privacy policies:

  • RevenueCat - Subscription management (App Store purchase data only)
  • Stooq - Stock prices (ticker symbols only)
  • Alpha Vantage - FX/crypto prices (ticker symbols only)
  • OpenAI - Optional AI insights (anonymized portfolio summary, never raw data)
