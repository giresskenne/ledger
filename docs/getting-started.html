Quick Start Guide

1. Choose Your Tracking Intent

Select what you want to track: stocks, cryptocurrency, real estate, cars, savings, or other assets. This helps Ledger customize the experience for you.

2. Add Your First Asset

Tap the "+" button and enter details about your asset. For stocks/crypto, you can search by ticker symbol for automatic price updates. For other assets, manually enter the value.

3. Set Pay Frequency (Optional)

If you have regular contributions like a 401(k) or monthly crypto purchases, set your pay frequency. This data is encrypted and never leaves your device.

4. Track Your Progress

Watch your dashboard update with real-time market data. View performance charts, allocation breakdowns, and net worth trends.

Understanding Your Dashboard

  • Total Value - Your complete net worth across all assets
  • Performance - Overall gain/loss and percentage return
  • Allocation Chart - Visual breakdown by asset type or individual holdings
  • 24h Change - How your portfolio performed today
