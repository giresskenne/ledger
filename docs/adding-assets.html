Adding Assets

How to add stocks, crypto, and other investments

Adding Your First Asset

Tap the "+" button on your dashboard to add a new asset. Follow these steps:

  1. Select Category - Choose the asset type (stocks, crypto, real estate, etc.)
  2. Name Your Asset - Enter a recognizable name
  3. Search Ticker - For stocks/crypto, search by ticker symbol (optional)
  4. Enter Details - Add quantity, purchase price, and current value
  5. Save - Your asset will appear on your dashboard

Ticker Search

How it works: When you search for a ticker symbol, Ledger queries market data APIs to find matching stocks or crypto. Select from results to auto-populate price data.

Can't find your ticker? You can skip ticker search and manually enter values. This works for any asset.

Editing Assets

Tap any asset on your dashboard to view details. From there you can:

  • Update quantity or purchase price
  • Change asset category
  • Add notes or tags
  • Delete the asset

Best Practices

  • Use ticker search for stocks/crypto to get automatic price updates
  • Add purchase price to track gains/losses accurately
  • Update manual assets quarterly (real estate, vehicles)
  • Group similar assets by category for better allocation insights
  • Include all holdings for a complete net worth picture
