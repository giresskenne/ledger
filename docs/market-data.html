Market Data

Understanding live prices and data sources

How Market Data Works

Ledger fetches real-time market prices for stocks and cryptocurrencies using industry-standard APIs. When you add an asset with a ticker symbol, the app automatically queries these data sources to keep your portfolio values current.

Update Frequency

  • Stocks: Updates every 5-15 minutes during market hours
  • Crypto: Real-time updates (prices refresh every 1-3 minutes)
  • FX Rates: Daily updates, typically around market open
  • Manual Assets: Only update when you manually change the value

Market Hours

US Stock Markets: 9:30 AM - 4:00 PM ET, Monday-Friday (excluding holidays)

Cryptocurrency: 24/7 trading, prices update continuously

Pre-market/After-hours: Ledger shows last close price until regular hours resume

Data Accuracy

We use reliable, industry-standard data providers, but prices may have slight delays (typically 5-15 minutes). For critical trading decisions, always verify prices with your broker.

Important: Ledger is a portfolio tracking tool, not a trading platform. Price data is for informational purposes only.

Supported Markets

  • US Markets: NYSE, NASDAQ, AMEX
  • International: LSE (UK), TSX (Canada), ASX (Australia), and more
  • Crypto: Bitcoin, Ethereum, and 1000+ altcoins
  • ETFs: All major US and international ETFs
  • Mutual Funds: Most US mutual funds (end-of-day pricing)

Currency Conversion

Ledger automatically converts foreign assets to your selected base currency using daily FX rates. Choose your preferred currency in Settings > Currency Selector.

Supported Currencies: USD, EUR, GBP, CAD, AUD, JPY, and 50+ more

Troubleshooting

Prices not updating?

  • Check your internet connection
  • Pull down to refresh the dashboard
  • Verify the ticker symbol is correct
  • Some delisted or OTC stocks may not have data

Wrong price showing?

  • Ensure ticker symbol matches exactly (e.g., "AAPL" not "Apple")
  • For international stocks, use proper exchange suffix (e.g., "BP.L" for London)
  • Report incorrect data to contact.corpltd@gmail.com
